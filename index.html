About the SymTermes

The forth edition of Symposium of Termitology (SymTermes) will be held from November 8th to 10th, 2017, at the Federal University of Vicosa, in Vicosa Minas Gerais, Brazil. The event is aimed for both to undergraduate and graduate students, professionals and companies. The main objective is to promote scientific disclosure and debate enabling the information exchange between the different areas of termites’ research.

Postgraduate Program in Entomology

Viçosa

Viçosa is naturally hospitable. Focusing its activities on the Federal University of Viçosa, UFV, the city receives people from various parts of Brazil and the world either for a quick stay to take part in academic-scientific events or for a longer period to expand one’s academic background and acquire further knowledge. Thus, if you wish to start a postgraduate program in Entomology at UFV, you’ll find here excellent hospitality.

Despite being small, Viçosa is a cosmopolitan city. Living in Viçosa means being constantly part of an exchange program. The variety of people living here confers to the city a high cultural diversity. With a permanent population of over 76 thousand inhabitants and a floating population estimated in 15 thousand people, Viçosa offers highly distinct lifestyles, depending on what you intend to experience here.

Climate

The climate in Viçosa is tropical of altitude, with mean annual temperature of 18.5 °C, varying between 8.2 °C and 28.5 °C. However, its climate has sudden oscillations, and some times of the year people experiences thermal sensation of the four seasons in one day.

For the event’s dates, it is expected temperatures between 25 and 30°C during the day, as in the previous years. Moreover, at that time of the year, it commonly rains during the night and the temperature decreases at dawn (around 19°C).
